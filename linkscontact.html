Links

Below are some websites that I have found to be particularly useful at one time or another.  Please enjoy.

Mathwords: Terms and Formulas from Beginning Algebra to Calculus
Bruce Simmons (2011).  A great website for looking up mathematical terms and formulas

Illustrated Mathematics Dictionary
Rod Pierce (2012).  A website for looking up mathematics terms.

National Library of Virtual Manipulatives
Utah State University (2010).  A site for online manipulatives.

MathType
Design Science (2012).  They have a wonderful tool for writing equations in Microsoft Word and PowerPoint that is easier to use than Equation Editor.

LaTeX
A great tool in preparing documents with embedded mathematics.

Graphing Calculator
Pacific Tech.  This is a wonderfully powerful tool for creating diadatic objects in mathematics classrooms.

Wolfram Math World
Eric W. Weisstein (2012). A constantly evolving mathematics resource.

Arizona State University
A public, R1 university located in Tempe, AZ, that offers a wide variety of undergraduate and graduate programs.  This is where I'm currently working on my PhD.

Northwest Missouri State University
A midsized, public university located in Maryville, MO that offers undergraduate and graduate programs.  I completed my Master's degree here.

Doane University
A small, private, liberal arts college located in Crete, NE offering undergraduate degrees and a few select graduate programs.  This is my alma mater....GO DOANE FORENSICS!!
